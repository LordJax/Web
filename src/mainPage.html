Hydrus - A Discord Bot

About

Hydrus is an fully user friendly bot that can be made to your will! You can shutoff/active commands, systems, etc. Change the bots messages, colors, Name, its all customized to you! With are new system if your bot leaves or it gets kicked do not worry! Once you invite the bot back it goes right back to how it was like nothing ever happend before, and with are cross user system you can have your data move over (like your partner/the person your married to. Your friends, etc!) With this bot. You Will never need to manage your discord EVER!

The default set of modules includes and it's not limited to:

  • Moderation features (kick/ban/softban, filters, mod-log...)
  • Music features (playlists, youtube, soundcloud, queues...)
  • Stream alerts (twitch/hitbox)
  • Imgur/gif search
  • And much much more
